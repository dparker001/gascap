Understanding the Placard Codes

Each placard has a unique tracking code printed on the back. Here's how to read it:

ORL003C
││└──── C = Counter  (P=Pump, W=Window, F=Flyer)
│└───── 003 = Location #3 in your territory
└────── ORL = Orlando

This code is how GasCap™ tracks scans, app opens, and sign-ups back to your specific placements — and to you. Never swap codes between locations.

After Each Placement — Report It

  1. Place the placard and get the consent form signed.
  2. Take a photo of the placard in place (proof of placement).
  3. Open the reporting form: Submit Placement Report →
  4. Fill in: placard code, business name, address, owner contact info, placement type, photo upload.
  5. Hit submit. Done. Your placement is logged and counted toward your monthly reward.

⚡ Submit the form immediately after each placement — while the details are fresh. It takes about 90 seconds.
